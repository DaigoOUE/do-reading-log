  - [Steady-State Quantum Zeno Effect of Driven-Dissipative Bosons with Dynamical Mean-Field Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.03191):  
  駆動されるBose-Hubbard modelにおけるzeno効果の解析にDMFT
  - [Dynamical Mean Field Studies of Infinite Layer Nickelates: Physics Results and Methodological Implications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.02852):  
  ニッケル酸化物系の物質中の電子状態の解析にDMFT
